## Implementation Notes for Course Designers

### Data Privacy & Consent

Before administering this survey, ensure:

- Students understand how their data will be used
- Students can opt out of personalization
- Sensitive data (Section 9, accessibility) is stored securely
- Students can update their responses during the course

### Customization Guidelines

**Section 6 (Prior Learning)** must be customized for each course. Include:

- 3-5 major topic areas from your syllabus
- Questions about formal/informal learning sources
- Self-rated understanding levels
- Practical experience questions
- Known concepts/skills (checklist format)

### Scoring Prior Experience

Combine Section 6 responses to create experience levels (1-5):

- **Novice (1):** No formal learning + no practical experience + low self-rating
- **Beginner (2):** Some exposure + minimal practical experience
- **Intermediate (3):** Formal course OR significant self-study + some practice
- **Advanced (4):** Multiple learning sources + substantial practice + high self-rating
- **Expert (5):** Extensive training + professional/significant project experience

### Optional Additions

Consider adding course-specific questions about:

- Previous courses in related subjects
- Software/tools familiarity
- Specific prerequisite skills
- Disciplinary vocabulary knowledge
- Previous instructors or teaching styles that worked well
